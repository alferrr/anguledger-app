About

Anguledger is a lightweight expense tracker built with Angular. It helps users record, manage, and review daily expenses in a simple and distraction-free way.

How to Use Anguledger

1. Add an Expense

  • Navigate to the Dashboard Page
  • Quick Add Card is seen in the left part of page beside Total Expenses
  • Enter the name, amount, category and date
  • Click Add Expense

2. View Your Expenses

  • All expenses are displayed in a chronological list
  • Recent entries appear first
  • Scroll to review your spending history

3. Delete an Expense

  • Select an expense from the list
  • Click the Trashbin icon at the leftmost side of the list

4. View Reports

  • Open the Reports page
  • See summaries of your spending
  • Use this to better undestand your financial habits

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally in your browser
  • No account or internet connection required
  • Clearing browser data will remove all saved expenses
